Welcome to Tegucigalpa, Honduras

nestled among montains and bisected by the choluteca river, Tegucigalpa is a city steeped in history, offering breathtaking views and vibrant culture. known for the imposing picacho montain, its traditional neighborhoods, and its diverse cuisine, the capital of Honduras has something for everyone
